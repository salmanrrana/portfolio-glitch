# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Hiring managers, technical peers, collaborators, and curious visitors evaluating Salman R Rana through a memorable portfolio experience. They arrive from search, social, resumes, or project links and need a fast sense of craft, engineering range, and personality.

## Product Purpose

Present Salman R Rana as a software engineer through a scroll-driven, full-viewport portfolio that feels authored rather than templated. Success means visitors remember the interaction, can reach contact links without friction, and can inspect projects without losing the portfolio context.

## Brand Personality

Restless, cinematic, technical. The site should feel like a controlled system glitching into new rooms, not a generic developer landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid beige startup pages, generic SaaS card grids, terminal cosplay, stock portfolio templates, and flat project lists that feel disconnected from the hero world.

## Design Principles

- Let the video world drive the interface; overlays should feel like they are emerging from the same physical scene.
- Use distortion as navigation, not decoration.
- Keep the primary path simple: name, contact, projects, then project inspection.
- Make motion intense for users who want it and calm for users who request reduced motion.
- Preserve performance and accessibility even when the visuals get strange.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-reachable navigation, visible focus states, and meaningful labels. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` with simplified transitions and no WebGL dependency for core content access.
