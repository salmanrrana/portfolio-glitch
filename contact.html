Brain Dump

View repository

SDLC for agent-driven software work.

Brain Dump uses engineering concepts people already trust: projects, epics, tickets, acceptance criteria, comments, review findings, demos, linked files, and telemetry. The difference is how the work gets completed. AI agents can move through the same quality workflow no matter which LLM provider is doing the work.

  • Provider-agnostic workflow for Codex, Claude, Cursor Agent, OpenCode, Pi, and other agents.
  • Ticket lifecycle from backlog through implementation, AI review, human review, and done.
  • Context, cost, review, and work history captured beside the work instead of buried in chat.

Maa Faa Notes

Open app

Capture notes first, let AI organize the work later.

Maa Faa Notes is for the moments when opening a chat window is the wrong move. It gives me a place to jot down anything, lets agents read the notes with context, and keeps track of what the AI contributes back into the notebook.

  • Daily capture and shared notes reduce the need to prompt from scratch.
  • Agent contributions make AI activity visible instead of letting it disappear into a transcript.
  • Notes can become Brain Dump epics and tickets to solve now or save for another day.
